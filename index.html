About Company

Building Sustainable Engineering Solutions!

Dependable Mechanical Systems Inc. (DMS) was incorporated in August 2002. The company started construction work in 2005 and has ever since grown exponentially in the province of Ontario, both in quality and the size of construction work it undertakes. DMS currently operates from a state of the art 25,000 sq. ft. facility at its head office in the City of Vaughan. The company also has an Ontario East Regional office in Belleville from where it services the Eastern region from Kingston to Ottawa.

DMS has successfully completed several Design Build, LEED and energy retrofit projects in the ICI sector. In the years 2010 & 2011 DMS was listed by the Profit Magazine amongst the top 10 fastest growing companies in Canada and has further been listed in the Profit 200 and Profit 500 for three consecutive years thereafter. The Vaughan Citizen, Construction Today, Advantage Magazine, The Globe and Mail and the Canadian Business Journal have all showcased DMS’s growth in their published articles over the last few years.

Featured Project

Check and enjoy our featured project

Union Pearson Express Union Station Platform

Mechanical Contract Value: $3,140,000.00

UP Express is is a $23 million Metrolinx project awarded to EllisDon Corporation. It is an innovative rail link service that will connect the country's two busiest transportation hubs, Toronto Pearson International Airport and Union Station in downtown Toronto, with each one-way trip taking approximately 25 minutes. Taking to the tracks in time for the Pan Am and Parapan Am Games in summer 2015, the service will combine the easy convenience of rail travel with graceful design and superior service.

The Mechanical scope consists of supply and installation of boilers, water heaters, split A/C units, humidifiers, snow melting system, in-floor heating system, exhaust fans, air curtains, heat recovery ventilators, air handling units, plumbing fixtures and all pipe and duct work including insulation and controls.
